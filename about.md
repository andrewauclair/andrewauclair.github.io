Hi there!

My name is Andrew Auclair and I am currently a Software Engineer at Tactical Communications Group, A Curtiss-Wright Company in Tewksbury, Massachusetts. I am a graduate of Champlain College in Burlington, Vermont where I received a Bachelor of Science Degree in Game Programming. Since graduating college I have branched out and left the game industry, but I still maintain the passion for writing exceptional quality software and going the extra step that I learned during my time in college and in the game industry.

My passion as a programmer has been and continues to be learning. I am always looking for the better approach to a problem and the better ways to implement clean code. Currently I am learning as much as I can about Test Driven Development to improve the code I write. I am also very passionate about developing tools that enhance pipelines and work flows.
